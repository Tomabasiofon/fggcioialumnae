WELCOME

We are Federal Government Girls College, Ikot Obio Itong Alumnae Association. We are a non-profit body connecting all our alumnae and giving back to our Alma Mater. We've setup programmes targeted at today's alumnus as well as active students of the school. Follow the link below to view our projects.

FGGC IOI Alumnae

We connect alumnae with their Alma Mater and each other, support reunions, organize events, and promote FGGC Ikot Obio Itong's legacy and reputation as one of the highly recognised government secondary schools in Nigeria. Our members aim to network, support one another, and find opportunities to give back to the school throuh mentoring, scholarships, excellence awards, infrastructural projects, etc.
