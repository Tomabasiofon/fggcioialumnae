Career Day

Blog

11th of August, 2022, the Alumnae Assciation hosted the school graduating class to a career day packed with wholesome talk sessions covering topics such as career choices/ paths, dealing with peer pressure, the challenges of life after school, etc. The resource persons included Chioma Odocha & members of the Association Executive Committee- President Uwem Martins, Vice President Ayoge Bassey, Secretary-General Henrietta Ayaogu, Financial Secretarty Ekomobong Etuk, Treasurer Ndifreke Asuquo Etim, Welfare Coordinator Udoro Daniels.
